AI Visibility Engine
Must ship before Dhul Hijjah 1447, then continuously iterated and updated. Get HDF ranking in AI search results — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini & Google AI Overviews. Lucky owns this end-to-end as a developer building systems and pipelines, not content.
Owner: Lucky Full-stack + LLM engineering Pre-Dhul Hijjah → Continuous GEO / AEO Figma MCP HDF AI Bot
Task 01
AI Citation Tracker
Scheduled bot that fires target donor queries at ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini & Google AI Overviews. Logs full responses, detects HDF mentions, surfaces a weekly trends dashboard. Establishes baseline and measures progress over time.
Task 02
Schema & Structured Data Injection
Programmatically generate and inject JSON-LD schema across HDF's entire site — Organization, Charity, FAQPage, Donation schemas. Signals authoritatively to AI crawlers who HDF is, what it does, and where it operates. Pure engineering work.
Task 03
Programmatic SEO Engine
Build a system generating hundreds of targeted static pages at scale — /give-zakat-for-orphans, /sadaqah-jariyah-water-well-pakistan. Lucky builds the engine, templates, and deployment pipeline. Optimised for AI engine discovery, not just Google.
Task 04
Figma MCP Integration
Integrate Figma via MCP so design assets, components, and brand guidelines feed directly into HDF's development workflow. Enables design-to-code pipelines and keeps AI-generated content visually on-brand with HDF's design system.
Task 05
HDF AI Bot
Maintain, extend, and iterate on the existing HDF AI Bot — expanding its knowledge base, improving response quality, and connecting it to new data sources. The bot serves as a live donor-facing touchpoint and a signal engine for GEO content gaps.
